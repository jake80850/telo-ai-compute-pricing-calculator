Vast.ai Notes

Vast.ai charges an ~8% platform fee — factor this into your rate input above. You need a reliable 1Gbps+ internet connection — ideally 10GbE. Run Ubuntu 22.04 with their Docker setup (they provide a script). Pricing fluctuates — peak demand (evenings, weekends) can be 30–50% higher than off-peak. The 32GB+ VRAM threshold is important: it unlocks 70B+ LLM workloads that command premium rates. New GPUs take a few weeks to build rental history and ratings before reaching full utilization.
